What is ECO?

ECO stands for Electronic Component Optimization, our goal is manage, reuse and fix broken electronic components from devices to use them on another ones. Reducing the utilization of materials to make them.

Benefits of reusing components

  • Reduction of electronic waste
  • Saving natural resources
  • Encouraging innovation and creativity
  • Contributing to environmental care

Goals and objectives

Reduction of electronic waste

By reducing electronic waste, we are not only decreasing environmental harm but also reducing the number of components that need to be manufactured.

Encouraging Component Reuse

By reusing electronic components, we can reduce the need to manufacture new parts, helping to save resources and lessen the environmental impact.
